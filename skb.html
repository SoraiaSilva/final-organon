Smart Kiosk Bot: how to improve human experiences in retailers using a chatbot.

ABOUT

The Smark Kiosk Bot is the answer to assist retailers by improving the client experience throughout a retail journey, with a virtual bot for support. This idea allows to improve the customer experience by having a personalized assistance 24/7 and a continuous experience in any platform possible, in or out the store.

CONTEXT

The fast pace of technology growth had a huge impact on the retail industry. Shoppers now have access to endless information about products and the proliferation of personal devices such as the smartphones are changing customer's shopping experience. This poses a great opportunity, but also a huge risk, as retailers have access to massive amounts of customer and market data and may adapt their approach. The technology growth is creating a shift on consumer preferences regarding the way they purchase their goods. A lot of people now tend to prefer e-commerce over brick-and-mortar businesses and this preference grows as time passes by.

CHALLENGES

The retail industry is still the biggest one when it comes to selling goods or services directly to consumers and retailers want to keep it that way. In order to do that, it is important to focus on improving consumer’s in-store experience whilst providing and suggesting a broader range of products online and assisting throughout the process.

  • >  The retail industry still strongly depends on selling goods or services directly to consumers;
  • >  Focusing on improving consumer’s in-store experience is important;
  • >  Binding in-store journey with online resources is a priority.

SOLUTION

  • >  “Smart Kiosk Bot” - the answer to better assist retailers improving client experience.
  • >  Virtual bot support for personal support, in and out of the store.
  • >  Personalized assistance 24/7.
