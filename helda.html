HELDA - Helpful Disassembly Assistant

Peugeot Citroën Mangualde

HELDA aims to develop an application capable of helping its users in operations such as disassembly/repair in automobile workstations, alongside with the registration of occurrences for future reference and analysis.

This project takes advantage of the Android's voice recognition system and turns it into a continuous listening service, capable of identifying a set of keywords and acting according to them.

HELDA is a complete solution, that eliminates errors in the disassembly process, minimizes downtime while providing data that can be used for process refinement.

OVERVIEW


The PSA group is present across the entire automotive value chain. It is comprised of strong iconic brands such as: Peugeot, Citroen, Opel, Vauxhall, etc. Their main goal is to provide its customer with unique automotive experiences that bring them freedom and pleasure.



CONTEXT

HELDA is a group of applications designed to streamline and facilitate the process of car disassembly for factory workers of the Peugeot/Citroën group. It will also provide a front end interface for technicians to visualize and analyze statistics related to the disassembly process. This interface will allow the detection of any flaws in the disassembly, thus facilitating the efficiency maximization of the whole process.

Disassembling > Packing > Shipping > Reassembling


CHALLENGES

  • > Great diversity in models of vehicles
  • > Necessity for the mechanics to memorize instructions for each vehicle model
  • > Large investment in training
  • > Significant number of mistakes are registered
  • > No way to catalogue and detail errors in the disassembly process
  • > No way to collect data about time inefficiencies in the disassembly process

    1. Eliminate errors in the disassembly process

    2. Minimize downtime

    3. Provide data that can be used for process refinement

    4. Streamline the disassembly process



SOLUTION

  • > Reads out instructions to the worker
  • >Voice controlled
  • > Register anomalies
  • > Measure elapsed times for each task by factory worker/car model
