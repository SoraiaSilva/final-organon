Beyond Sight

ITGROW | CRITICAL Software

The goal of Beyond Sight is to allow people with visual impairments to access nonverbal information.
This mobile application, through the use of audio description, will communicate with its users, providing a way of understanding the contents shown in a Powerpoint, thus enabling them to autonomously attend those types of presentations without any assistance.

3 main components:

An extension for Powerpoint, able to inform the user about the content presented in the current slide;

A server that allows registration of presentations and mobile clients;

A mobile application where the information that is displayed on the current slide will be transmitted in real time.

These three parts would work together to provide visually impaired people with the tools to follow a presentation in a much less frustrating way. The application provides real-time information of changes to slides and of changes regarding the content of the slides (appearance and disappearance of text blocks, for example), and is accessible to users of screen readers, as well as compatible with desktop and mobile devices.

HELDA - Helpful Disassembly Assistant

Peugeot Citroën Mangualde

HELDA aims to develop an application capable of helping its users in operations such as disassembly/repair in automobile workstations, alongside with the registration of occurrences for future reference and analysis.

This project takes advantage of the Android's voice recognition system and turns it into a continuous listening service, capable of identifying a set of keywords and acting according to them.

HELDA is a complete solution, that eliminates errors in the disassembly process, minimizes downtime while providing data that can be used for process refinement.

Pomar

LGP

“POMAR” is a portuguese acronym, standing for Produtos Orgânicos, Marítimos, Agrícolas e Rurais. This project aims to develop a mobile app for Portuguese people and local producers, where users can get more information about local farmers’ markets and its products.

However, POMAR is more than a location based app. It is designed for the modern consumer, concerned with what they eat and how to make a stand in local communities, taking care of the environment.

Pomar promotes engagement between consumers and producers in an easy and user friendly way.

Portugal is a perfect starting point:
1) 8% of portuguese worry about the hard effects of food activities
2) Top 10 countries with the highest growth of organic agricultural land in 2015
