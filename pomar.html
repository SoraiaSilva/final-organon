Pomar

LGP

“POMAR” is a portuguese acronym, standing for Produtos Orgânicos, Marítimos, Agrícolas e Rurais. This project aims to develop a mobile app for Portuguese people and local producers, where users can get more information about local farmers’ markets and its products.

However, POMAR is more than a location based app. It is designed for the modern consumer, concerned with what they eat and how to make a stand in local communities, taking care of the environment.

Pomar promotes engagement between consumers and producers in an easy and user friendly way.

Portugal is a perfect starting point:
1) 8% of portuguese worry about the hard effects of food activities
2) Top 10 countries with the highest growth of organic agricultural land in 2015
